# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- Financial counsellors running client meetings in desktop-first environments.
- Product and engineering stakeholders using the app as a realistic workflow demo.
- End users who need fast transcript review, client linking, and action extraction without backend complexity.

## Product Purpose

This product demonstrates an AI-assisted financial counselling meeting workflow from recording to structured follow-up. It simulates chunked ASR and diarization behavior, keeps review and linking as explicit user steps, and presents generated insights in a way that feels production-ready.

Success means users can:
- move through `idle -> recording -> processing -> reviewing -> linking -> summarized` without confusion,
- understand what AI is doing at each phase,
- trust that transcripts and summaries are actionable and easy to verify.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, operational.

The interface should feel:
- calm under pressure,
- precise and enterprise-ready,
- helpful without hype.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI SaaS visuals (purple gradients, flashy glow-first UI, decorative glass effects).
- Marketing-style hero gimmicks inside product surfaces.
- Overloaded loading panels with too many simultaneous status widgets.
- Cute or toy-like interaction language that weakens professional trust.

## Design Principles

1. **Workflow first**: Every surface should make the next user action obvious.
2. **Trust through clarity**: AI states are explicit, legible, and never mysterious.
3. **Professional restraint**: Motion and color support comprehension, not decoration.
4. **Review before commit**: Users can pause, revise, and confirm before downstream AI actions.
5. **Consistent system voice**: Similar components and states should look and behave consistently across the flow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive controls.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for non-essential animation.
- Keep touch and click targets clear and sufficiently large where applicable.
- Maintain bilingual parity (zh/en) for key flow guidance and status labels.
